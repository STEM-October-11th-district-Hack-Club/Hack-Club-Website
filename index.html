Welcome to STEM October 11th District Hack Club!

Hack Club is a global nonprofit organization that empowers high school students to become makers, coders, and creative problem solvers through community-led coding clubs and events.

Here at STEM October 11th District Hack Club, we believe in the power of learning by doing. We're a student-led club where creativity, curiosity, and code come together to build real-world projects, solve meaningful problems, and have fun along the way.

What We Do

Our club hosts a variety of hands-on activities — from beginner-friendly workshops to full-scale hackathons — where members can collaborate, build, and showcase their skills.

Every week, our members dive into coding challenges, design sessions, and collaborative projects that stretch their skills and spark new ideas. Whether you're a total beginner or an experienced coder, you'll find a place to learn, build, and grow.

We also organize events like the STEM School Hackathon, themed workshops, and build days where members create websites, games, apps, hardware hacks, and more.

Why Join Hack Club?

  • Build awesome projects with other creative students
  • Learn by doing - no lectures, no grades, just pure building
  • Join a supportive, fun, and ambitious coding community
  • Get access to mentorship, funding, and real-world opportunities
  • Take part in workshops, hackathons, and team-based challenges
